Our Story

A Space Born for Your Finest Moments

Falcon Theaters is a premium private theatre and party hall located in Bangalore, designed exclusively to make your intimate gatherings unforgettable.

As a proudly women-owned and LGBTQ+ friendly establishment, we believe in creating safe, joyous spaces where everyone can be their authentic selves. Our venue combines luxurious aesthetics with exceptional hospitality.

Our attentive team is dedicated to perfecting every detail—from elegant decor to flawless audiovisual experiences—ensuring your celebration shines the brightest.
